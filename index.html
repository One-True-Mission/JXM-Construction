About JXM

A Texas Crew.

JXM Construction LLC is a full-service construction company based around Shiner, Texas, proudly extending our work into the Houston, Austin, and San Antonio areas. We handle every part of the build, from the first nail to the final coat of paint.

Whether you're tearing down to the studs or breaking ground on something brand new, we bring the same attention to detail, the same pride in workmanship, and the same straightforward communication. No surprises. No subcontractor runaround. Just work that lasts.
