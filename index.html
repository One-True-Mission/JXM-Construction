About JXM

A Texas Crew.
A Texas Standard.

JXM Construction LLC was founded in 2025 by Jordan Mesa, but the experience behind it goes back a whole lot further. Jordan grew up on construction sites watching his dad run a construction business, learning the trade hands-on from the time he was old enough to swing a hammer. Over a decade later, JXM brings that foundation to every project.

Based around Shiner, Texas, we proudly extend our work into the Houston, Austin, and San Antonio areas. Everything from framing to finish work is handled by our in-house crew, with only concrete and masonry subbed to trusted specialists. The result is tighter schedules, cleaner communication, and one team accountable for every part of the build.

What We Do

Full-Service Construction

Six core areas of expertise, one phone call. From frame-up to final coat, we're your single point of contact.

⌂
New Construction

Ground-up builds and home additions, managed start to finish. From the first stake in the ground to the final inspection, we handle framing, structural work, and permit coordination. One crew, one timeline, no subcontractor shuffle.

✦
Remodels

Kitchens, bathrooms, and whole-home renovations done right. We handle layout reconfiguration, cabinets, countertops, and every detail in between. Tired spaces brought back to life with materials and craftsmanship built to last.

▤
Interior Finish

The details that decide whether a space feels finished or feels cheap. Drywall installation, tape and mud, texture, interior and exterior paint, tile, and hardwood flooring. All done clean, all done tight.

◈
Plumbing & Electrical

In-house rough-in and finish work for remodels and new builds. No waiting on outside trades, no coordination headaches, no surprise delays mid-project. Wiring, fixtures, plumbing systems, all handled by the same crew on your job.

▥
Doors, Windows & Fences

Installation that seals tight and looks right. Interior and exterior doors, window replacement, and custom fence builds. Optional fence staining and finishing to bring the whole exterior together.

⌂
Roofing & Outdoors

Built to handle everything Texas weather throws at it. Roof replacements and repairs, siding installation, patios, and outdoor structures. Weather-rated materials and finishes that hold up year after year.
