Meet The Owner

Jordan Mesa, Owner & CEO

JXM stands for Jordan, and Jordan stands behind every project. Quality, customer satisfaction, and execution are what we focus on, while striving for perfection on every build.

JXM Construction LLC was founded in 2025, but the experience behind it goes back much further. Jordan grew up on construction sites. His dad started a construction business and showed him the ropes from the time he was old enough to swing a hammer. Framing, finish work, plumbing, electrical. The whole trade, learned the way it should be learned: hands-on, job by job, year after year.

That foundation matters. When you've spent a lifetime watching how things are built right, you can also see when they're being built wrong. And you don't let that happen on your job.

After more than ten years in the field, Jordan launched JXM to build something of his own. The name carries his initials, but the standard carries his father's lessons: do the work right the first time, treat the customer like family, and never leave a job you wouldn't be proud to put your name on.

Based around Shiner, Texas, JXM proudly extends services into the Houston, Austin, and San Antonio areas. Whether the project is a kitchen remodel two towns over or a ground-up build three hours out, the standard doesn't change. Most construction outfits sub out everything past framing. JXM keeps it in-house: drywall, tape and mud, paint, tile, flooring, plumbing, electrical, doors, windows, fences, and roofing. Concrete and masonry are the only trades we sub, and we sub to specialists we trust.

The result is tighter schedules, cleaner communication, and one crew accountable for every part of the build. No subcontractor runaround. No "that's not our guy" excuses. Just the work, done right, by the people you hired.
